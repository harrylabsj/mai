# Mai (买) - Smart Shopping Assistant

A comprehensive shopping assistant that helps users research products, compare prices, find deals, and make informed purchasing decisions.

## Capabilities

1. **Product Research** - Search and gather information about products
2. **Price Comparison** - Compare prices across different platforms
3. **Deal Finding** - Identify discounts, coupons, and promotions
4. **Review Analysis** - Summarize user reviews and ratings
5. **Purchase Decision Support** - Provide recommendations based on user needs and budget

## Usage Scenarios

- "我想买一台笔记本电脑"
- "帮我比较一下 iPhone 15 的价格"
- "这个商品值得买吗？"
- "找找有什么优惠活动"
- "分析一下这款产品的评价"

## Workflow

1. Understand user's purchase intent and requirements
2. Research products matching the criteria
3. Compare prices across platforms
4. Analyze reviews and ratings
5. Provide purchase recommendations

## Output Format

- Product summary with key features
- Price comparison table
- Review highlights
- Recommendation with reasoning
- Purchase links (if available)

## Notes

- Supports major e-commerce platforms
- Focuses on publicly available information
- Does not handle actual transactions or payments
